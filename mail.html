Tech Tuesdays is a knowledge sharing session among TWers and a jump-starting platform for budding speakers.

Topics range anything from one's learning/ experiences/ practices / innovations to practically anything that you want to talk about and has a bearing on technology.

Tech Tuesdays is a weekly talk and as the name suggests, happens on a Tuesday.

It started in the month of April and we already had more than 20 topics spanning over the areas Programming languages, Infrastructure, Problem Solving, Testing, Front-end, Back-end, etc. Since we hear from a lot of you that you watch the recordings post the sessions, we’ve come up with a monthly Summary to make your lives easy.
